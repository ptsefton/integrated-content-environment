span.T1 {font-style:italic; font-weight:bold; }</style></head><body><p>USQ<i>Connect</i> provides you with online access to information, services and course resources relevant to your studies from a convenient, central point. To access USQ<i>Connect</i>, from the USQ home page at &lt;<a href="http://www.usq.edu.au/">http://www.usq.edu.au</a>&gt; click on USQ<i>Connect</i>, or go directly to the URL at &lt;<a href="http://usqconnect.usq.edu.au/">http://usqconnect.usq.edu.au</a>&gt;. You will require your USQ<i>Connect</i> username and password to access the system. You will be notified of this username and password on your first Letter of Offer or Enrolment Notice.</p><p class="P2"><b>Study Desk</b></p><p class="P3">Your Study Desk in USQ<i>Connect</i> gives access to a home page for every course in which you are currently enrolled. Content available from the course home page will vary according to the teaching requirements of the course, but may include: </p><ul class="lib"><li><p>course materials and resources</p></li><li><p>electronic discussion facilities</p></li><li><p>access to past examination papers.</p></li></ul><p><br/>As each course has specific learning requirements, availability of these features will vary between courses.</p><p class="P2"><b>USQ</b><b><i>Admin</i></b> </p><p>USQ<i>Admin</i>, also accessed through USQ<i>Connect</i>, allows you to access a number of administrative functions such as changing your contact details, checking your enrolment details, accessing learning circles, checking final grades, viewing your exam timetable, changing your exam centre, and more.</p><p class="P2"><b>Other links</b></p><p>USQ<i>Connect</i> also gives access to the Library and the Academic Learning Support site, as well as the Quick Links list of University sections and services.</p><p/></body></html>
